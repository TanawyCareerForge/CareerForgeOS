# CareerForge OS Mission

**Version:** 0.1.0-alpha  
**Status:** Draft  
**Purpose:** Define the operational mission for CareerForge OS.

CareerForge OS exists to turn verified professional information into reusable career intelligence that people and organizations can trust.

## Responsibilities

- Provide reusable career knowledge primitives.
- Enable explainable AI workflows for career documents, interviews, and coaching.
- Support open-source extensibility through agents, engines, SDKs, and plugins.

## Dependencies

- Vision
- Architecture
- Documentation standards

## Inputs

- Verified user history
- Job and role requirements
- Career goals

## Outputs

- Career intelligence artifacts
- Explainable recommendations
- Validated documents and workflows

## Validation Rules

- Do not infer unverifiable achievements as facts.
- Separate user facts, system suggestions, and external context.

## Examples

- Convert work history into a verified skill graph.
- Generate a coaching plan with evidence links.

## Test Cases

- Confirm generated outputs distinguish facts from suggestions.
- Confirm recommendations reference source inputs.
