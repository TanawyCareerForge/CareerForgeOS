# CareerForge OS Vision

**Version:** 0.1.0-alpha  
**Status:** Draft  
**Purpose:** Define the long-term product vision for CareerForge OS.

CareerForge OS will become the world's first AI-native Career Intelligence Operating System: a complete, trustworthy, explainable ecosystem that helps people understand, communicate, and grow their professional value.

## Responsibilities

- Establish the north star for product, architecture, governance, and community decisions.
- Protect truthfulness, explainability, and human-first career outcomes.
- Keep the platform model-agnostic and enterprise maintainable.

## Dependencies

- Project mission
- Architecture specification
- Governance framework

## Inputs

- User-provided career facts
- Labor-market context
- Recruiter and ATS expectations
- Enterprise compliance requirements

## Outputs

- Product direction
- Scope boundaries
- Roadmap priorities

## Validation Rules

- The system must never fabricate user facts.
- Recommendations must be explainable.
- User dignity and agency must take precedence over automation.

## Examples

- A user can understand why a resume bullet was improved.
- An enterprise can audit how an agent reached a recommendation.

## Test Cases

- Verify every major product module maps to the vision.
- Verify roadmap items do not conflict with truth-first principles.
